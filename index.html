About

I am an industrial designer, born in the middle of the world in a small and beautiful country called Ecuador, I have worked as a professor in a public and private university for the past 2 years, teaching about the development of industrial product design.

Currently, I'm a PhD candidate in the Design, Manufacturing, and Management of Industrial Projects program at the Universitat Politécnica de Valéncia.

I used to be in charge of a Fab Lab 3 years ago and it was when I realized that I wanted to be involved in this network and for this reason I am joining the Fab Academy program to learn more about digital manufacturing technologies and how to apply it. In hand rehab devices, that is part of the subject I am working on in my research thesis.
